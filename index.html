Smart ATS Chatbot

Your AI-powered resume analysis and career guidance assistant

About the Project

The Smart ATS Chatbot is an AI-powered application designed to help job seekers refine their resumes, receive actionable feedback, and explore career opportunities tailored to their skills. With features like resume scoring, personalized feedback, and internship/job recommendations, this tool simplifies the job search process for students and professionals alike.

Features

  • Upload resumes for AI-based analysis
  • Receive personalized feedback and resume scores
  • Discover relevant internships and job opportunities
  • Chat with the AI for career guidance and improvement suggestions
